Changing the Tooltip Text

Hover over the input field to see the tooltip.

This is the editable text.

The tooltip is designed to provide users with guidance as to how an inline-editable field can be used. The default text is "Select or press Enter to edit," but you may wish to change that to somethind different.

Mark-up:

<div id="test">
    <div class="flc-inlineEdit-text">
        <div class="flc-inlineEditable">This is the editable text.</div>
    </div>
</div>

Code:

var opts = {
tooltipText: "Yo, click on me! Or whack the
              Enter key, if that's your thing."
};
var myInlineEdit = fluid.inlineEdit("#test", opts);
